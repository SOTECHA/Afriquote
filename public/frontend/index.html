We built this because
we felt these problems

Every feature in AfriQuote exists because African professionals were losing time, money, and clients to problems that generic tools never solved.

01
Payment delays killing cash flow
Over 77% of Nigerian freelancers report payment delays due to informal invoicing. AfriQuote's auto-chasers and mobile money integration get you paid 3× faster.
→ Payment chaser + Paystack/Flutterwave
02
Scope creep eating your profits
"Just one more thing" from clients costs African professionals an average of 20% of project revenue. Our scope tracker flags every out-of-scope request in real time.
→ Scope tracker + change orders
03
Tax compliance confusion
VAT rates differ across Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, and South Africa. Withholding tax surprises. Filing deadlines missed. AfriQuote handles all of it automatically.
→ Multi-country tax dashboard
04
No visibility on remote sites
Managing projects in Lagos while a site stalls in Kigali. Permits lapsing without notice. Weather halting work with no documentation. Solved with live site monitoring.
→ Site monitor + field log
05
Undercharging for your work
Without time tracking, African professionals lose track of hours and undercharge by up to 30%. Our live timer and billable rate calculator show exactly what you're worth.
→ Time tracker + rate calculator
06
Proposals going cold with no follow-up
Sending a proposal and waiting passively costs African professionals 30-40% of winnable deals. AfriQuote's proposal pipeline tracks every prospect from first contact to signed contract.
→ Proposal pipeline + follow-up alerts
07
Western tools that don't fit
QuickBooks integrates with US banks. Xero doesn't know about WHT. Stripe doesn't work in Nigeria. AfriQuote is built from the ground up for African financial infrastructure.
→ Local payments + local compliance
