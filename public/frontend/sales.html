Nigeria NTAA 2025

Are you WHT exempt?
Most freelancers don't know.

The Nigeria Tax Administration Act 2025 introduced a WHT exemption for freelancers earning below ₦2 million per month who have a valid TIN. The calculator checks this automatically — and tells you exactly what to say to corporate clients who try to deduct WHT incorrectly.

✓
Exempt if: Monthly income below ₦2,000,000 AND you have a valid, active TIN registered with FIRS. Show your TIN certificate when a client attempts to deduct WHT.
✗
Not exempt if: You have no TIN, or your monthly income exceeds ₦2M. Corporate clients must deduct WHT at 10% for services. Always request a WHT credit note for your annual tax return.
